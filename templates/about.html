Our Mission

Garuda Aerospace is revolutionizing the mining industry by combining advanced drone technology with artificial intelligence to provide real-time, accurate insights into mining operations. We empower mining companies to optimize resources, improve safety, and make data-driven decisions that increase profitability and sustainability.

Our platform uses cutting-edge 3D mapping technology (OpenDroneMap) and AI algorithms to analyze mining sites, calculate accurate volume measurements, and generate comprehensive reports in minutes instead of days.

Our Vision

To become the global standard for mining intelligence, where every mining operation leverages AI and drone technology to achieve maximum efficiency, safety, and sustainability. We envision a future where mining companies have complete visibility into their operations, enabling smarter decisions, reduced costs, and minimal environmental impact.
